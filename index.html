This chart compares Life Expectancy versus GDP per capita of 182 nations from the years 1800 to 2014. Each bubble represents a country, and the size of each bubble represents the population with the color representing the region.

Controls

  • To play or pause the visualization press the play button on the top right.
  • Use the continents selector to narrow the data down to specific continents.
  • Use the countries checkboxes to choose specific countries and add labels.
  • Clear countries and continents filters with the clear filter button on the top right.
  • Use the slider to go to specific years.
  • Reset the visualization with the Start Over button
